This App is designed to generate stock price predictions using both ARIMA and LSTM models for any company listed under S&P 500. User is asked to enter the company symbol of their choice. Symbols can be obtained from the following link. Daily stock prices are imported from the Alpha Vantage API in json format. Having obtained the data, a time series plot (blue) showing the evolution of the company's stock prices is generated. To test the validity of the models, the dataset is split into training (80%-blue) and test (20%-green) sets. Both ARIMA and LSTM predictions (yellow) are superimposed on top of the test set to highlight model inaccuracies.

The trading strategy implemented in our app is based on the behavior of short and long term moving avergaes (MA). It is assumed that the momentum usually lies with short term MAs. Hence, whenever the short term MA crosses the long term MA, a trading signal is generated. If the former overtakes the latter the investor is advised to sell his/her shares while buy signals take into effect if the reverse is true. Finally, an equity curve based on the aforementioned trading strategy shows the net profit made by the investor.
